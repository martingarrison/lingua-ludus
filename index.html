Lingua Ludus

A focused flashcard app for building Spanish vocabulary from the ground up. Cards are drawn from the SUBTLEX-ESP frequency list — the 2,000 most common words in everyday spoken Spanish.

The algorithm surfaces high-frequency words you haven't seen yet, while spacing out cards you already know. Study a little every day and the words will stick.
